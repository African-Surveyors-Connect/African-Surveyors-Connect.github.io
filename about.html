Organization Story

What started off as a platform for performing surveying calculations late in the year 2019, ended up being a community platform for the African community in the fields of Geomatics and Surveying

The idea was to help students easily calculate and understand Surveying field calculations.
In March 2020, the platform expanded and a Blog section was added.

Ever since then, the community has managed to add its contributions to the idea hence formulating the African Surveyors Community.

Some of the components that consist of African Surveyors Connect are:

  • Blog Section
  • COVID-19 Resource Center
  • Careers Portal
  • Surveying Applications
  • Classifieds Section
  • E-Learning & YouTube Tutorials
  • Publications Portal

The platform is still growing and we aim to be the largest network of Surveying and Geomatics students and professionals across Africa
